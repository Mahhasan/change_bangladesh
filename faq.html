1. What is the Change Bangladesh Award?

The Change Bangladesh Award is a recognition that aims to celebrate and honor individuals or organizations that have made a significant positive impact in their communities, industries, or the world at large through their work, initiatives, or projects, in certain specific areas like education, healthcare, communication, technology, innovation and entrepreneurship ecosystem, etc. .

2. Who can apply for the Change Bangladesh Award?

The Change Bangladesh Award accepts nominations from individuals, organizations, or third-party endorsers who wish to recognize the achievements and contributions of an individual or organization that has made a positive impact in their field or community.

3. What are the criteria for the Change Bangladesh Award?

The Change Bangladesh Award recognizes individuals or organizations that have demonstrated exceptional leadership, innovation, creativity, and dedication in making a positive impact on society or the environment. Nominees should have a track record of tangible and measurable results, impact already on ground, as well as a long-term vision for their work, and are willing to work further collaboratively to take their mission ahead.

4. How can I nominate someone for the Change Bangladesh Award?

To nominate an individual or organization for the Change Bangladesh Award, please visit the program's website and follow the instructions on the nomination form. You will need to provide information about the nominee's achievements, impact, and relevant background.

5. What is the selection process for the Change Bangladesh Award?

The selection process for the Change Bangladesh Award involves a thorough review of all nominations by a panel of judges who are experts in the fields related to the award categories. The details of the shortlisted nominees, including their films, reports and other documentation, shall then be placed before the final set of senior judges who shall evaluate the nominees based on the criteria of the award. The final winners shall be selected based on the senior judges' scores and recommendations.

6. What are the award categories for the Change Bangladesh Award?

The award categories for the Change Bangladesh Award have been mentioned in the website. You may follow those.

7. What is the prize for the Change Bangladesh Award?

Typically, each winner shall receive a trophy or plaque, media coverage, and recognition at an award ceremony or event. They also receive a monetary prize of 100,000 taka, and other benefits, such as mentorship or networking opportunities.

8. When is the deadline for nominations for the Change Bangladesh Award?

Please visit the program's website for the most up-to-date information on deadlines and other program details. The deadline for 2023 is expected to be August 31, 2023.

9. Is there any contact information for the Change Bangladesh Award?

In case you have any doubt,, please contact to sa.bd@daffodilvarsity.edu.bd

10. Can anyone awardee apply twice for the same award?

If anyone has received an award for a project, then he shall not be able to apply twice for the same award. But he would be able to apply for different categories.

11. Is there any age limit for the Award?

No, there is no age limit for the Award. We just want to promote good work and encourage others to do the good work.

Learning Future Conclave & Awards (LeFCA), Dubai, 2023

Celebrating Ideas that are Changing Education of the Developing World

25-27 October, 2023

Movenpick Grand Al Bustan, Dubai

Register Here

Why this Conclave?

(A) Education has gone through a revolutionary change due to the pandemic and its aftermath owing to disruption in earlier paradigms of learning.
(B) Technology leapfrogging is paramount now and is ushering in a new universe of learning, with challenges of digital divide evident too.
(C) Informal and diverse educational routes have come to stay. Alternative learning, home schooling, open education etc are becoming common place today.

What is

Change Bangladesh ?

ChangeBangladesh.net is a platform to create an ecosystem of positive impact change-makers, their diverse contributions, amplifying them, and taking them to the global level. There are so many people in our country & outside of our country who are contributing to various sectors but do not get the recognition. Change Bangladesh wants to identify the most impactful such change-makers and work for and with them.

More About Registration

Mission

The mission of ChangeBangladesh.net is to develop sustainable strategies that empower individuals, communities and governments to drive positive transformation. This includes creating economic development opportunities; promoting social justice initiatives through education, health services and other programs, and identifying and honoring positive change makers of Bangladesh.

Vision

Find out the change makers & recognize them toward the world to inspire people to make good contributions of positive impact. Making the world a better place by good deeds.

What can be our awarding process?

Awarding through a befitting event, one Best Performing & Impactful Change-maker of Bangladeshi origin, annually, with 1,00,000 taka, scroll and memento, selecting them through a transparent jury-based global search process within and beyond Bangladesh, in the diverse areas.

How can we celebrate those Change Makers?

A changemaker is an individual that has identified a social challenge, developed a solution and put a group together to solve the problem in practice. We need to celebrate those change makers to appreciate the good deeds. Events & awards can be the best way to celebrate them.
